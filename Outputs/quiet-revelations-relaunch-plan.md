# Quiet Revelations — Safe Relaunch Plan

**Goal:** Rebuild the channel in a way that avoids future strikes and grows a genuine audience.

---

## Phase 1 — Before You Upload Anything (Week 1)

- [ ] Wait for appeal result before creating a new channel
- [ ] If appeal is denied, create a fresh channel with a new name (or same name)
- [ ] Set up channel art, description, and About section — make it look established
- [ ] Add a channel trailer (even 30 seconds of text + voiceover introducing the channel)
- [ ] Link a real email in the contact section — signals legitimacy to YouTube

---

## Phase 2 — Fix What Got You Taken Down (Before First Upload)

### AI Content Disclosure
- On every video upload, go to: Details > Content Declaration > "Contains AI-generated content"
- Enable this toggle — no exceptions

### Titles — Drop the "Hidden Truths" Framing
Avoid phrases like:
- "The Full Story They Never Told You"
- "What They Don't Want You to Know"
- "Secrets Hidden in Scripture"

These trigger misinformation filters. Use instead:
- "The Garden of Eden — A Deep Study of Genesis 1-3"
- "Who Was Cain? The Full Biblical Account"
- "Noah's Ark — What the Bible Actually Says"

Same content. Much safer framing.

### Descriptions
- Always include the scripture references used (e.g., Genesis 2:7, 2:15)
- Add a disclaimer: *"This channel uses AI-assisted tools for voiceover and visuals, disclosed in accordance with YouTube policy."*

---

## Phase 3 — Upload Schedule (Slow & Steady)

**Do NOT upload 3 videos in the first week.** New channels that upload in bursts look like content farms.

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Upload 1 video. Promote it. Engage with comments. |
| Week 2 | Upload 1 video |
| Week 3 | Upload 1 video |
| Week 4+ | 1-2 videos per week max |

---

## Phase 4 — Build Trust Signals

YouTube rewards channels that look real. Do these:

- **Reply to every comment** in the first 48 hours of each video
- **Add end screens and cards** to every video (links to next video)
- **Create a community post** after each upload — share a quote from the scripture covered
- **Create Shorts** — clip 30-60 seconds of the most powerful narration from each video. Shorts get discovered faster and feed subscribers to your main channel.

## Growth Tips for This Niche

- **Thumbnail style:** Dark/dramatic background, bold white text, one powerful image (cross, fire, dramatic sky)
- **Hook rule:** First 30 seconds must grab attention — open with a question or a surprising fact
- **Length:** 8–15 minutes performs best in Bible/faith content
- **Competitor channels to study:** Devotional Dude, Wisdom for Dominion, Bible Stories Explained
