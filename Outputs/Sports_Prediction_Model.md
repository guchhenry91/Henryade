**Value Edge Formula:**
> Value Edge % = Model Probability % − Implied Probability %
> Implied Probability % = 100 / (decimal odds)
>
> **Positive edge = value bet. Only bet A-tier with edge > +5%**

## LEAGUE-SPECIFIC FACTORS

### EPL
- Home/away record (some teams struggle away significantly)
- Fixture congestion — rotation risk
- Managerial tactics (press vs low block)
- Set piece threat (top aerial teams)
- Key props: goals, shots on target, cards
- **Correct score tip:** 1-0 and 1-1 are statistically the most common EPL scorelines. Low-scoring games common mid-table. Top 6 home games lean 2-0, 2-1.

### UEFA Champions League
- Away goals mentality in two-legged ties
- Fatigue from domestic schedule
- Squad depth for rotation
- Historical performance in knockout rounds
- Key props: goals, clean sheets, scorer anytime
- **Correct score tip:** Group stage — 1-0, 2-0 common for stronger sides at home. Knockout legs — 1-1 and 0-1 high value if away side is strong.

### La Liga
- Possession-heavy teams inflate pass stats
- Real Madrid / Barcelona home dominance
- Midweek fatigue factor
- Key props: goals, assists, corners
- **Correct score tip:** Real Madrid / Barcelona home games frequently end 3-0, 2-0, 3-1. El Clasico historically tight — 1-0, 2-1 most common. Bottom-half clashes lean 1-0, 0-1.

### NBA
- Home court advantage (~60% win rate historically)
- Back-to-back games — fatigue/rotation impact
- Pace of play (affects over/under lines)
- Matchup exploitation (e.g. slow center vs fast PG)
- Key props: points, rebounds, assists, 3PM, PRA combos

### NFL
- Weather conditions (wind/rain affects passing games)
- Line movement — sharp money indicator
- Divisional games tend to be closer
- Home underdog value (covers spread more often)
- Key props: passing yards, receiving yards, TDs, rushing yards
