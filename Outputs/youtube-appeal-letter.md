# YouTube Channel Appeal Letter — Quiet Revelations

**Use this in YouTube's channel termination appeal form.**
**Keep it factual, calm, and brief. Do not be emotional or aggressive.**

---

## Appeal Text (Copy & Paste)

Subject: Appeal — Quiet Revelations Channel Termination

My name is [Your Name] and I am the owner of the YouTube channel "Quiet Revelations."

I am respectfully appealing the termination of my channel. I believe this decision may have been made in error, and I would like to provide context.

**About the channel:**
Quiet Revelations is a faith-based educational channel dedicated to exploring the stories of the Bible in depth. All content is original, scripture-based, and created with the sole purpose of helping viewers engage more deeply with the Word of God.

**Videos published:**
- The Garden of Eden — The Full Story (Genesis 1-3)
- Cain and Abel (Genesis 4)
- Noah's Ark (Genesis 6-9)

**Regarding AI-generated content:**
Some of my videos use AI-assisted tools for voiceover and visuals. I understand that YouTube requires disclosure of AI-generated content, and I take full responsibility for not including that label on my uploads. This was an oversight, not an attempt to deceive. I am committed to properly labeling all future content in compliance with YouTube's updated policies.

**My content does not:**
- Violate community guidelines on hate speech, violence, or harmful content
- Contain misinformation — all narration is grounded in scripture and widely accepted biblical scholarship
- Promote any harmful ideology, product, or agenda

I am a small creator genuinely passionate about sharing faith-based content. I respectfully ask that you review my channel and consider reinstating it. I am fully willing to re-label any content, make edits, or take any corrective action required.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

[Your Name]
[Your Email]
[Channel URL if available]

## Where to Submit

1. Go to: https://support.google.com/youtube/troubleshooter/2990837
2. Select: "My account or channel was terminated"
3. Fill in the form and paste the text above
4. Be patient — appeals typically take 7–30 days

---

## Tips
- Submit only ONE appeal. Multiple appeals can hurt your case.
- Do not create a new channel while the appeal is pending — YouTube can detect it and deny the appeal.
- If denied, you can escalate via the YouTube Creator Support Twitter/X account @TeamYouTube.
