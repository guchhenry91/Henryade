## HOW TO VERIFY A JOB IS DIRECT (NOT AGENCY)

- URL goes to company's own domain (e.g. careers.sig.com, jobs.bendingspoons.com)
- Hosted on Greenhouse/Workday/Ashby/Lever/Pinpoint under company's name
- LinkedIn posting shows company logo + "Posted by [Company]" not a recruiter name
- No mention of "our client" or "on behalf of" in the job description
- Company has its own careers page listing the same role
